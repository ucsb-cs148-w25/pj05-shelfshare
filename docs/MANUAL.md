## Book Club Page

<img width="1673" alt="Screenshot 2025-03-17 at 12 58 12 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/112a1426-ff74-419b-bceb-c7f16902ee0e" />

- You can create book club. 
  When you create a club, you would be able to...
  1) Choose club's name
  2) Write club info
  3) upload club's image
  4) assign a book that the club will be reading
  5) assign a due date for reading chapters
  * Note that you could edit this information after you create your club by clicking edit club button on your club's page.
  * Only creator of the club can modify these information
    
  <img width="563" alt="Screenshot 2025-03-17 at 12 59 23 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/21cf8f04-b981-47e4-b1dc-e4cbc3a49c63" />

- Search book club by their name
- Click on the club to join and start communicating with club members
- if you are the owner of the club, you would be able to
  1) modify club infor
  2) delete club
  3) Mark the completed books
  4) assign new book to read
     
  <img width="1158" alt="Screenshot 2025-03-17 at 1 07 35 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d13b0bd1-ab41-410f-97cd-5cd9de8697c1" />

## MyShelf Page

<img width="1677" alt="Screenshot 2025-03-17 at 1 17 48 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/355b08cc-1169-485e-9c13-059b7540b7ae" />

- You could add books to your shelves on book page.
- You could move moves from one shelve to another by dragging a book.
- Make sure that you move the book that you finished reading to the "finished reading" shelve to enable for you page AI recommendation feature. 
- You could create new shelve by clicking the new shelf button on the top of the page. This shelve will show up as one of the shelve to add books on the book page.
- Click on the book and it will take you to that book's information page.
- Click on the edit button next to the shelf name to delete books from the shelf.
